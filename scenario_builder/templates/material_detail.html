On this page you can see all aspects of how a material is defined.

Groups: When analysing a material, it can be devided in differert component groups (e.g. macro components or chemical elements). All components of each group must add up to 1 i.e. 100%. A group can refer to the whole material or to a component of the material. E.g. most measurements are made with respect to the total solid content of a material.

Components: Any component of a material can be measured with analytical methods. On this page, the compositions are given by weight fractions of the components the material consists of.
